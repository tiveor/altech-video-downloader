# Privacy Policy — Altech Video Downloader

**Last updated:** April 9, 2026

## Data Collection

Altech Video Downloader does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## How the Extension Works

- All video detection and downloading happens **locally in your browser**
- Network requests are monitored only to identify video streams — no data is sent to external servers
- No browsing history, personal information, or usage data is recorded

## Permissions

The extension requires certain browser permissions to function. These are used exclusively for video detection and downloading:

- **activeTab / host_permissions** — Used to scan the current page for video elements
- **webRequest** — Used to detect video streams in network traffic
- **downloads** — Used to save videos to your computer
- **scripting** — Used to inject the DOM scanner into pages
- **storage** — Used to store extension state locally
- **offscreen** — Used to process HLS streams into MP4 files

## Third-Party Services

This extension does **not** use any third-party analytics, tracking, or telemetry services.

## Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. Ever.
